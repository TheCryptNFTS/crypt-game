# FACTION DESIGN BIBLE

## STONE
Fortress midrange.
Wins by durable board presence, GUARD walls, CRUSH finishers.

## IRON
Aggro pressure.
Wins by early tempo, RUSH density, efficient curve pressure.

## BRONZE
Tempo skirmish.
Wins by fast trades, board pivots, pressure backed by efficient bodies.

## SILVER
Arcane control.
Wins by artifacts, defensive board patterns, slower value accumulation.

## GOLD
Elite value.
Wins by higher quality bodies, stronger top-end, selective power spikes.

## GLOBAL RULE
Faction identity must come from mechanics, not just stat packaging.
